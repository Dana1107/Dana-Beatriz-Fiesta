BUDDHA BOWL

A Plant-Based Protein-Iron Bowl with Vitamin C for more effectivity!

INGREDIENTS

1/2 cup Squash - boiled
10 Sweet Potato Leaves - boiled
1/4 cup Soy Bean Sprouts - boiled

1 cube Tofu - cubed into 27 cubes
1 small can of Button Mushrooms - sliced into halves
1/4 cup Black Beans

2 Tomatoes - cubed
10 Chinese Celery Leaves
1 cup Rice

FOR TOFU SAUCE:
     > 30g Oyster Sauce
     > 1/2 tbsp Lemon/Calamansi Juice
     > 1 tbsp Honey
     > 1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
     > 1/2 tsp Onion Powder
     > 1/4 tsp Ground Black Pepper - to taste

  1. STEP 1 - Heat pan over medium heat, put 1 tsp of olive oil or any vegetable oil. Saute 1 tbsp grinded garlic. When the garlic turns golden brown, simmer 1 cup water then boil with 1/2 cup soy bean sprouts. Sprinkle with salt and boil for 5 minutes. Hang and put the soy bean sprouts in a bown then put 1 tbsp sesame oil. Try it now!
  2. STEP 2 - Heat pan over medium heat, put 1 tsp of olive oil or any vegetable oil. Saute 1 tbsp grinded garlic. When the garlic turns golden brown, saute 1/2 cup mushroom along with it then put 1 tbsp of soy sauce, 1 tbsp sesame oil and sprinkle with sesame seeds. Hang and put in a dish after 3 minutes. Try it now!
  3. STEP 3 - Heat pan over medium heat, put 1 tsp of olive oil or any vegetable oil. Saute 1 tbsp grinded garlic. When the garlic turns golden brown, saute spinach along with it then put 1 tbsp sesame oil and sprinkle with sesame seeds. After 3 minutes, hang and put in the same dish beside mushroom.
  4. STEP 4 - Repeat step 3 but with carrot instead of spinach.


  5. STEP 5 - Repeat step 3 but with zuccini instead of spinach


  6. STEP 6 - With big mushroom, bath the 2 halves with soy sauce, sauted 1 tsp garlic, sesame oil and sesame seeds then heat them all in a pan for 5 minutes. After that, fry the mushroom halves with 1 tsp of olive oil. With tofu, do the same process as big mushroom.

  7. STEP 7 - Put 1 cup rice in the center of your desired bowl. Put soy bean sprouts, mushroom, spinach, carrot, zuccini, cucumber and kimchi around rice. Top the rice with big mushroom or tofu.ry it now!
